Mathematical Problem 1:

Problem:

Gerard starts watching One Piece, and the number of episodes he watches follows a Fibonacci sequence. Gerard watched 3 episodes on Day 1, 2 episodes on Day 2, and each follows the sequence.

How many total episodes will he have finished after 7 days?

On what day will he reach 29 episodes watched?

Show your solution here:

Given:

  • a₁ = 3
  • a₂ = 2

Formula: aₙ = aₙ₋₁ + aₙ₋₂

Solution:

a₃ = 2 + 3 = 5

a₄ = 5 + 2 = 7

a₅ = 7 + 5 = 12

a₆ = 12 + 7 = 19

a₇ = 19 + 12 = 31

Cumulative Total:

3 + 2 = 5

5 + 5 = 10

10 + 7 = 17

17 + 12 = 29

29 + 19 = 48

48 + 31 = 79

Gerard has watched a total of 79 episodes of One Piece in 7 days. He reached 29 episodes watched in total on Day 5.

Mathematical Problem 2:

Problem:

Bash starts playing Warframe with a Fibonacci pattern. On Day 1, Bash plays for 130 minutes, on Day 2, he plays for 40 minutes, and each day follows the sequence.

Between Day 1 and Day 5, which day has the highest minutes for gaming?

On what day will he first play more than 3 hours in a single day?

Show your solution here:

Given:

  • a₁ = 130
  • a₂ = 40

Formula: aₙ = aₙ₋₁ + aₙ₋₂

Solution:

a₃ = 40 + 130 = 170

a₄ = 170 + 40 = 210

a₅ = 210 + 170 = 380

Calculation to hours:

130/60 = 2.16 hours

40/60 = 0.66 hours

170/60 = 2.83 hours

210/60 = 3.5 hours

380/60 = 6.33 hours

Day 5 has the highest amount of minutes for gaming with 380 minutes, almost twice as much from the last. Bash will first play more than 3 hours on Day 4.

Mathematical Problem 3:

Problem:

Kuya Fan Club, a local band in Paranaque City, is having a week-long tour with one concert each day. They noticed they sell tickets in a Fibonacci pattern. In their first concert, they sell 45 tickets, and the one after they sell 75 tickets. Each concert's ticket sales are the sum of the last two.

How many tickets will they have sold on the last concert of the tour?

What is the total amount of tickets sold by the end of the tour?

When did they first sell more than 500 tickets?

Show your solution here:

Given:

  • a₁ = 45
  • a₂ = 75

Formula: aₙ = aₙ₋₁ + aₙ₋₂

Solution:

a₃ = 75 + 45 = 120

a₄ = 120 + 75 = 195

a₅ = 195 + 120 = 315

a₆ = 315 + 195 = 510

a₇ = 510 + 315 = 825

Kuya Fan Club sold 825 tickets on Day 7, the last day of their tour. The total number of tickets sold in this tour is 2085 tickets. They first reached the 500 tickets sold mark on their 6th concert where they sold 510 tickets.
