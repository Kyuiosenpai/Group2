Mathematical Problem 1: A rectangle has the length of 3x - 2 and the width of x - 4.

Find the expression for the area of the rectangle.

Show your solution here:

Formula: Area = Length x Width

Solution:

(3x - 2)(x-4)

3x • x = 3x^2

3x • (-4) = -12x

-2 • x = -2x

-2 • (-4) = 8

3x^2 - 12x - 2x + 8

3x^2 - 14x + 8

Final Answer: 3x^2 - 14x + 8

Mathematical Problem 2: A company sells a product for x^2 + 5x - 6 pesos per item and produces x + 3 items per day.

Find the total daily revenue in terms of x.

Show your solution here:

Solution: Revenue = price per item x number of items

(x^2 + 5x - 6)(x + 3)

x^2 • x = x^3

x^2 • 3 = 3x^2

5x • x = 5x^2

5x • 3 = 15x

(-6) • x = -6x

(-6) • 3 = -18

x^3 + (3x^2 + 5x^2) + (15x - 6x) - 18

x^3 + 8x^2 + 9x - 18

Final Answer: x^3 + 8x^2 + 9x - 18

Mathematical Problem 3: A ball is thrown into the air, its height after t seconds is given by:

h(t) = -5t^2 + 20t + 15.

Find the maximum height of the ball.

Show your solution here:

The height function is a quadratic equation in the form: ax^2 + bx +c

The maximum height occurs at the vertex, given by:

t = -b/2(-5)

with a= -5 and b = 20:

t = -20/2(-5) = 2

with t solved we can finally do the real equation:

h(2) = -5(2)^2 + 20(2) + 15

= -5(4) + 40 + 15

= -20 + 40 + 15

h(2) = 35

Final Answer: 35
