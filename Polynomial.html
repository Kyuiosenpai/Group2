Mathematical Problem 1:

Problem:

A rectangle has the length of 3x - 2 and the width of x - 4. Find the expression for the area of the rectangle.

Show your solution here:

Formula: Area = Length × Width

Solution:

(3x - 2)(x - 4)

Expanding:

3x × x = 3x²

3x × (-4) = -12x

-2 × x = -2x

-2 × (-4) = 8

Combining like terms:

3x² - 12x - 2x + 8

3x² - 14x + 8

Final answer: 3x² - 14x + 8

Mathematical Problem 2:

Problem:

A company sells a product for x² + 5x - 6 pesos per item and produces x + 3 items per day. Find the total daily revenue in terms of x.

Show your solution here:

Solution: Revenue = price per item × number of items

Expanding:

(x² + 5x - 6)(x + 3)

x² × x = x³

x² × 3 = 3x²

5x × x = 5x²

5x × 3 = 15x

-6 × x = -6x

-6 × 3 = -18

Combining like terms:

x³ + (3x² + 5x²) + (15x - 6x) - 18

x³ + 8x² + 9x - 18

Final answer: x³ + 8x² + 9x - 18

Mathematical Problem 3:

Problem:

A ball is thrown into the air, its height after t seconds is given by: h(t) = -5t² + 20t + 15. Find the maximum height of the ball.

Show your solution here:

The height function is a quadratic equation in the form: ax² + bx + c

The maximum height occurs at the vertex, given by:

t = -b / 2a

With a = -5 and b = 20:

t = -20 / (2 × -5)

t = 2

Substituting t = 2 into the height equation:

h(2) = -5(2)² + 20(2) + 15

= -5(4) + 40 + 15

= -20 + 40 + 15

h(2) = 35

Final answer: 35
