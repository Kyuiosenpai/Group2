Mathematical Problem 1: Arithmetic Sequence

Problem: Sebastian was assigned to examine how many seats were in each row of an auditorium. The first row had 40 seats, the second row had 42, and the third row had 44. How many seats would be in the 20th row?

Solution:

Given:

  • a₁ = 40 (First row seats)
  • d = 2 (Increase per row)
  • n = 20 (20th row)

Formula: aₙ = a₁ + (n - 1) × d

Calculation: a₂₀ = 40 + (20 - 1) × 2 = 78

Mathematical Problem 2: Arithmetic Mean

Problem: Insert 2 arithmetic means between 9 and 36.

Solution:

Given:

  • a₁ = 9
  • a₄ = 36
  • n = 4

Formula: d = (aₙ - a₁) ÷ (n - 1)

Calculation: d = (36 - 9) ÷ (4 - 1) = 9

Final sequence: 9, 18, 27, 36

Mathematical Problem 3: Arithmetic Series

Problem: Find the sum of the first 60 terms of the arithmetic sequence 6, 16, 26, 36, 46,…

Solution:

Given:

  • a₁ = 6
  • d = 10
  • n = 60

Step 1: Find the last term a₆₀ = a₁ + (n - 1) × d

Calculation: a₆₀ = 6 + (59 × 10) = 596

Step 2: Use sum formula Sₙ = (n / 2) × (a₁ + aₙ)

Calculation: S₆₀ = (60 / 2) × (6 + 596) = 18,060
