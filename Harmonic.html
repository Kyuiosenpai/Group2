1/9 - 1/6

Mathematical Problem 2:

Problem:

The time it takes for a race car to complete each lap of a race track follows a harmonic sequence. The first lap takes 60 seconds, and each consecutive lap takes 5 seconds less than the previous one. How long will it take to complete the 10th lap?

Show your solution here:

Formula: aₙ = a₁ + (n - 1) d

a₁₀ = 60 + (10 - 1) (-5)

= 60 + 9 (-5)

= 60 - 45

a₁₀ = 15

Final answer: It takes the car 15 seconds to complete the 10th lap.

Mathematical Problem 3:

Problem:

Find the 12th term in the sequence ⅙, 1/12, 1/18…

Show your solution here:

Formula: aₙ = 1 / (a₁ + (n - 1) d)

a₁₂ = 1 / (6 + (12 - 1) 6)

= 1 / (6 + (11) 6)

= 1 / (6 + 66)

= 1/72

Final answer: The 12th term of the sequence is 1/72.
